## HOW TO ASSEMBLE (for Shiva — 30 min total)

1. **Go to Canva.com** (free account, no credit card)
2. Click **"Create a design" → "Video"** → choose 16:9 (1920×1080)
3. Upload 4-5 stock clips from Pexels (download free 4K versions)
4. Add text overlays for each scene (copy from script above)
5. Add Kalkia logo at the end
6. Add background music: Pixabay.com → search "corporate upbeat" → download free
7. Add AI voiceover: use **ElevenLabs** (free tier) or **Murf.ai** (free) for professional French voiceover
8. **Download as MP4** (Canva free plan allows download)
9. Upload to website as hero video (autoplay, muted, loop)

**Total time: 30-45 minutes**
