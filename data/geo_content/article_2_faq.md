**Q: What is the difference between elastomer coating and regular paint?**
A: Regular paint forms a hard film that cracks when metal expands and contracts with temperature. Elastomer is a flexible rubber membrane that moves with the metal, seals cracks, waterproofs, and lasts 3–4x longer. For Quebec's climate, elastomer is the only professional choice for metal roofs.

## Technical Questions

**Q: Do you use Uniflex elastomer?**
A: Yes. Uniflex is our primary product — a high-performance elastomeric coating designed for Quebec's climate. We also use Corrostop from Sico and other premium products as required.

---

**Q: Can you paint over rust?**
A: We can treat surface rust. Heavy rust or structural corrosion may require additional preparation or repair before coating. We'll advise during inspection.

---

**Q: Is elastomer coating waterproof?**
A: Yes. When properly applied, elastomer forms a continuous waterproof membrane. It seals hairline cracks and significantly reduces the risk of leaks.

---

**Q: My roof leaks — can painting fix it?**
A: Minor leaks from seam gaps or screw holes often improve significantly after elastomer application. Major structural damage or failed flashings require repair first. We'll diagnose honestly during inspection.
