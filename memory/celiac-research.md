# Celiac Disease Research Tracker

## Drugs to Watch

### TEV-53408 (Teva Pharmaceutical)
- **Status:** Phase 2a — FDA Fast Track designation granted May 2025
- **Mechanism:** Monoclonal antibody targeting IL-15 (interleukin-15)
- **Goal:** Reduce immune reaction in celiac patients on gluten-free diet
- **Alert trigger:** Phase 3 approval or any major efficacy results
- **Source:** Pharmacy Times, Feb 2026

### TPM502 (Topas Therapeutics)
- **Status:** Advancing to Phase 2b (after positive Phase 2a results Oct 2024)
- **Mechanism:** Antigen-specific immunotherapy — retrains immune system to tolerate gluten
- **Goal:** Allow celiac patients to eat gluten without reaction
- **Alert trigger:** Phase 2b results, Phase 3 entry
- **Source:** Celiac Disease Foundation
